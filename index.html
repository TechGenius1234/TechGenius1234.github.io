Why I Dislike 2FA

Introduction

Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) is often praised for its security benefits, but for some, it introduces unnecessary complexity and frustration.

My Reasons

  • Inconvenience: Requiring a secondary device or app adds extra steps to basic tasks.
  • Device Dependency: Losing access to your phone can lock you out of your accounts.
  • Unreliable Systems: Delays in receiving codes or app malfunctions make 2FA unreliable.

Conclusion

While security is important, 2FA should not compromise user experience. There must be better, more user-friendly alternatives.
